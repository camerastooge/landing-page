An app to help you stretch... anytime, anywhere! Stretch & Release helps you with your daily stretching exercises by letting you know when to stretch, when to rest, and when to stop.
Your stretching workout assistant!
Stretch & Release was for use in physical therapy.
Stretch & Release's audio prompts let you know when to stretch, rest, and stop.
Stretch & Release works on iPhone or Apple Watch.
Stretch & Release remembers your last stretch settings.
I built Stretch & Release when I was in physical therapy and was struggling to keep track of my duration and reps. I hope that it helps you on your fitness journey! -Lucas, app creator
